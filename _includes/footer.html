One Company. Infinite solutions.

Everpay is an innovative open payments provider that specializes in Fintech technologies and customized commerce solutions. Headquartered in Canada, Everpay provides businesses with multi-currency payment solutions, POS devices, tokenization services and PCI validated Point to Point Encryption solutions. Everpay also provides merchants and banks with advanced fraud management services including AVS-only, CVV, 3-D Secure™, Facial recognition and industry leading fraud prevention technologies.
